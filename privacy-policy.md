# Privacy Policy for ClipboardShelf

**Effective Date:** April 28, 2026  

ClipboardShelf (“the App”) is designed to help users manage and organize clipboard content securely and efficiently. Your privacy is important to us.

## 1. Information We Collect

ClipboardShelf does **not collect, store, or share personal data on external servers**.

- Clipboard data (such as text, links, or copied content) is stored **locally on your device only**
- No account or login is required
- No personal identifiers are collected

## 2. Clipboard Access

The App accesses clipboard content only when:
- You explicitly copy content, or
- You open or interact with the app

Clipboard data is:
- Never transmitted to external servers  
- Never shared with third parties  
- Fully under user control  

## 3. Data Storage

All clipboard entries are:
- Stored securely using Apple’s local storage frameworks (e.g., Core Data)
- Kept only on your device  
- Permanently deleted when you remove them or uninstall the app  

## 4. Analytics & Tracking

ClipboardShelf:
- Does **not use third-party analytics**
- Does **not track users**
- Does **not use advertising SDKs**

## 5. Permissions

The App may request:
- Clipboard access (required for core functionality)

No unnecessary permissions are requested.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **not**:
- Sell user data  
- Share data with third parties  
- Use data for advertising or marketing  

## 7. Security

We follow Apple-recommended best practices to protect your data, including:
- Secure local storage
- No external data transmission

## 8. Children’s Privacy

ClipboardShelf does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Effective Date” at the top of this document.
